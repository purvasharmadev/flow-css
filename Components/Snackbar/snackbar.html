Snackbar

Snackbars are often used as a tooltips/popups to show a message at the bottom of the screen.

Note: Snackbar has set width : 50%; It takes 50% of the parent element.

Simple Snackbar

Can't send photo. Retry in 5 seconds.
< div class "snackbar" >
Can't send photo. Retry in 5 seconds.
< span > × < /span >
< /div >
